How ResQGrid Works

From incident detection to resolution in seconds.

1

Incident Detected

Auto sensors or staff reports trigger the system. CRISP AI calculates confidence score from keyword signals.

2

Mesh Propagation

Alert relays device-to-device — no single point of failure. All dashboards update in real-time via WebSocket.

3

Smart Dispatch

System auto-assigns the nearest, best-skilled responder. Countdown timer starts for accountability.

4

Live Tracking

Responder status tracked: Assigned → En Route → Resolving. Reporter receives real-time updates.

5

Resolution & Debrief

Incident resolved with full timeline, confidence history, and mesh relay log saved for review.
